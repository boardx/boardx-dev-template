Executive summary
The market is consolidating around AI-native collaboration. Pricing splits into freemium and seat-based tiers; the strongest differentiation is real-time AI on an infinite canvas.
Key findings
•3 of 5 competitors lack inline AI generation.
•Teams value live transcription and summaries.
•Onboarding friction is the top churn driver.
Recommendation
Lead with AI-on-canvas in positioning; bundle transcription into the team tier; cut onboarding to under 3 steps.
Summary
From 18 interviews, three personas emerged. The sharpest, most repeated pain is slow setup and fragmented tools blocking the first "aha" moment.
Personas
The Facilitator
Runs workshops; wants speed and templates.
The Builder
Turns ideas into specs; wants AI on canvas.
The Reviewer
Async feedback; wants comments + summaries.
Top pain points
•"Setup took 20 minutes before I could start." (12/18)
•"I jump between 4 tools for one meeting." (9/18)
•"Hard to find an old board later." (7/18)
Opportunities
Cut setup to under 3 steps, unify chat + canvas + files, and add semantic board search.
